The cities of Frankfort (State Capital), Lexington, and Slade are plotted on the map above. By clicking on the plotted points city specific information will be displayed. The hyperlinks in the paragraph will take the reader directly to the population data sources. The three cities on the map illustrate how close in proximity the Urban-Rural divide really is in Kentucky. As an outsider living in Lexington I can see how the confluence of these two worlds gives the city its character and charm.
